| Position | Beebe Role | Deity | Function | Register |

| 2 | Good Parent | **Thalor** | Si | The Anchor — the tested record, the ritual, the reliable history |
| 3 | Child (Puer/Puella) | **Caelis** | Ne | The Leap — creative reframe, possibility within structure |

*Shadow positions 5–8 are the shadow versions of positions 4–1 reversed.
The Opposing Personality mirrors the inferior. The Daemon mirrors the Hero.*

## RULES FOR CURSOR — DEITY USAGE

1. **Names are immutable.** Varkon is always Te. Aureliane is always Fe. Never swap.

2. **The deities appear in:**
   - Holy day observances (each holy day belongs to a specific deity)
   - Shadow work screens (the deity's name heads the shadow chapter)
   - The holy book (Eight Who Carry the Fire — deity as character)
   - Planner shadow prompts (one deity per week, in Beebe order)
   - Doctrine tab deity reference card (all 8 deities, their functions, the ESFJ or INTJ position)

3. **The Tending app uses Aureliane where the Imperium uses Vaelith.**
   Both apps reference all eight deities but in different positions.

4. **In app UI, deity names appear:**
   - In the shadow work chapter headers: "Aureliane — The Hero" (Tending) / "Vaelith — The Hero" (Imperium)
   - In holy day titles: "The Day of Thalor" (Tending holy day for Si function)
   - In the weekly shadow prompt: "This week: Sereth — The Question"
   - In the Doctrine tab deity card

5. **Never invent new deity names. Never rename existing ones.**
   If a position requires a deity not on this list, you have miscounted the functions.
