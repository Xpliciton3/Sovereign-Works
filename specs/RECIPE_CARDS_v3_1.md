## PANTRY STAPLE: P-01 | HOMEMADE OAT-SEED GRANOLA
`NF` `GF` `DF` `VEG`

**Active time:** 5 min | **Bake:** 22 min | **Makes:** ~3 cups | **Stores:** 2 weeks airtight

> Replaces purchased certified granola throughout. Costs roughly $0.30/cup vs $2.50+/cup.

**INGREDIENTS:**

|------|--------|

| Sunflower seeds (raw, NF) | ¬º cup |

| Olive oil | 2 tbsp |

**METHOD:**
